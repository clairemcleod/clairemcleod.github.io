Hello! I'm Claire.

Right now, I'm a graduate student in bioengineering at Penn, where I work with the Mauck Lab. My research combines statistical learning with experimental biology to predict how individual cells will behave.

I've become increasingly interested in the use of large datasets to answer interesting questions. My research focuses on biological questions, but I also really enjoy working on other projects for fun. Some of my favorites are featured below. Most recently, I've looked at how runners' self-predicted performance matches up with their actual results.

Prior to graduate school, I went to Olin College, where I studied engineering with a concentration in bioengineering. During this time, I investigated how cells interact with their environment, in the context of blood vessel formation.
